# Crypto Hunters 6.1 Profit Predictor

The Profit Predictor ranks current Bitget USDT perpetual setups and returns either:

- one best qualified continuation setup, or
- **NO TRADE** when no market passes the filters.

It combines multi-timeframe Hunter score, RSI/MACD agreement, EMA structure, extension risk, liquidity, and ATR-based movement estimates. Confidence and movement zones are estimates, not guarantees.
